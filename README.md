2023-2024 NBA Season Data Analysis Dashboard
Project Overview
This project features a comprehensive interactive dashboard built with Power BI to analyze the 2023-2024 NBA season performance. The dashboard provides deep insights into both Regular Season and Playoff games, offering a data-driven perspective on player and team performance metrics.
Key Features
Comprehensive Data Coverage: Analyzes statistics for 572 unique players across 30 teams.
Player Performance Analysis: Evaluates metrics such as minutes played, total points, and performance distribution by age group (ranging from 19 to 39 years old) and position.
Team Performance Insights: Compares team statistics, including total points, field goal percentages, blocks, steals, assists, and rebounds.
Comparative View: Allows users to filter and toggle data between the Regular Season and the Playoffs to identify performance trends.
Interactive Design: Includes dedicated pages for "Intro," "Players," and "Teams" to facilitate a seamless navigation experience through the data.
Analytical Objectives
The dashboard was designed to answer critical questions, including:
Who are the top performers in terms of minutes played and total points?
How are total points distributed across different player positions?
Which teams lead in scoring, field goal percentages, and overall game performance?
How do performance metrics differ between the Regular Season and the Playoffs?
